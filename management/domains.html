Domain management

Within the oxide.one subsystem, there are a number of planned and unplanned releases and services. These services are planned to be exposed to the public, so that they are reachable everywhere.

Two domains are at the heart of the oxide.one system.

Production is the domain oxide.one

Pre-prod/testing is nitrate.one

Domains

I try to split up my domains into separate categories to make them easier to parse.

There are 6 categories that are currently in use.

Simple services

These are simple services. Generally these are services that do not require a database and can be considered 'stateless' (to some extent)

Media services

Media related services. This covers services that search, organize and download torrents. This is only for legal purposes. 

Authorization services

Authentication and authorization related services.

Utilities

These are various utilities that don't quite fall under 'stateless' services but provide some sort of utility.

Public

Public services that I provide to friends and family.
