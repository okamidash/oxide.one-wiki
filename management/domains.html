Domain management

Within the oxide.one subsystem, there are a number of planned and unplanned releases and services. These services are planned to be exposed to the public, so that they are reachable everywhere.

Two domains are at the heart of the oxide.one system.

Production is the domain oxide.one

Pre-prod/testing is nitrate.one
